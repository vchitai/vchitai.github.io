Wuzhen, China

Wuzhen is a historic scenic town, part of Tongxiang, located in northern Zhejiang Province, China. It lies within the triangle formed by Hangzhou, Suzhou and Shanghai. Wuzhen’s area is 71.19 km². The town has a total population of 60,000, of which 12,000 are permanent residents.
